Ontario Police College – Law Prep Cheat Sheet

1. Criminal Code of Canada

Arrest & Use of Force:

  • s. 25 – Use of force (lawful, necessary, reasonable)
  • s. 26 – Excessive force = criminal liability
  • s. 27 – Force to prevent crime
  • s. 31 – Breach of peace arrest
  • s. 495 – Peace officer arrest without warrant
  • s. 497 – Must release unless public interest

Common Offences:

  • s. 266 – Assault
  • s. 267 – Assault with weapon / bodily harm
  • s. 322 – Theft
  • s. 348 – Break and enter
  • s. 430 – Mischief
  • s. 320.14 – Impaired operation
  • s. 145 – Fail to comply with court order
2. Highway Traffic Act (HTA)
  • s. 216 – Driver must stop for police
  • s. 217(2) – Refusal to stop = offence
  • s. 48.1 – Stunt driving & vehicle impound
  • s. 130 – Careless driving
  • s. 172 – Racing / stunt driving
  • s. 33(1) – No licence
  • s. 218.1 – Arrest authority under HTA
3. Provincial Offences Act (POA)
  • s. 1 – Defines Part I, II, III
  • s. 8 – Service of offence notice
  • s. 9 – Notes must be complete and accurate
  • Understand PON completion and when to issue
4. Liquor Licence and Control Act (LLCA)
  • s. 42 – Open liquor in public or vehicle
  • s. 56 – Arrest for intoxication in public
  • s. 62 – Removal from licensed premises
5. Cannabis Control Act
  • s. 10 – Possession limits (age 19+)
  • s. 13 – Underage possession = offence
  • s. 21 – Arrest powers for certain offences
6. Youth Criminal Justice Act (YCJA)
  • s. 6 – Extrajudicial measures preferred
  • s. 56 – Enhanced rights to counsel/privacy
  • s. 146 – Youth statements & admissibility
Study Tips
  • Flashcards – one section per card
  • Practice real-world legal scenarios
  • Study groups & mock tests
  • Use CanLII or PDF law downloads
